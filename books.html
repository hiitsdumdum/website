Benevolence


an anthology.


Benevolence is not just a book on love or passion. Its the creation of various forms of love expressed through action, care, wishes, dreams, memories, relations, etc. This book is a dream come through of all the co-authors.


What is an anthology?

An anthology is a collection of literary pieces by various different authors.


A note from me: It was wonderful to get my work published in a book! Participating in this anthology wasn't just a life-changing but also a learning experience for me. I got to meet so many talented writers and learn about so many various writing styles in the process. This book contains the thoughts of not just one but many people, which enables us to wonder how the world looks like from different perspectives. I would really appreciate if you bought this book. Thank you!
